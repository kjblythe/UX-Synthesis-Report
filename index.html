Executive Summary

Usability Testing Results

In April 2026, BI X conducted in-person moderated usability testing with 7 GLP-1 patients at a professional research facility. Participants completed 6 structured tasks simulating a full day with the app. The prototype was a fully interactive Lovable-built PWA with live AI. This follows validated survey research, HCP interviews, and A/B testing.

1

Overwhelmingly positive response

Post-session satisfaction averaged 6.6/7. SEQ task ease averaged 6.4/7 — well above the 5.5 benchmark. All 7 expressed willingness to use the app daily.

2

AI nutrition features drove strongest engagement

Photo-based meal logging was the breakout — 6/7 chose it unprompted, with genuine surprise at recognition accuracy.

3

Primary gap is visual styling, not architecture

Interactive elements blend into the dark aesthetic — a contrast issue confirmed by the participant with lowest tech comfort who navigated every feature intuitively.
